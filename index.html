FAQ

Straight answers before we start.

How fast can you deliver?

A focused landing page can usually move faster than a full website, especially when content, feedback, and decisions are ready. After reviewing your scope, I will give you a realistic timeline instead of a vague promise.

Do you only work with businesses in Bhuj or Kutch?

No. I am based in Bhuj, Kutch, Gujarat, but I can work with clients remotely across India and worldwide.

Can you write the website copy too?

Yes. I can help structure and write the website copy so the page is clear, direct, and focused on enquiries. If you already have copy, I can refine it during the build.

Do you redesign existing websites?

Yes. I can improve the structure, messaging, design quality, mobile layout, speed, and conversion flow of an existing website.

Do you handle both design and development?

Yes. I handle the website direction, design approach, and frontend build together, which keeps the work cleaner and reduces handoff issues.

What do you need to get started?

Share your business details, current website if you have one, main goal, target audience, services or offer, timeline, and a few reference websites if you have them.

Do you offer maintenance after launch?

Yes. I can help with updates, small fixes, new sections, content changes, and ongoing improvements after the website goes live.
